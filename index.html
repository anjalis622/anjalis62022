Indian Cricket Team Salary & Grading points Analysis



Indian Cricket Team Players Salary: BCCI (Board of Control for Cricket in India) alloted the salary for the Indian cricketers (both Men and women) based on their performance which reflects as their points to get positioned in the BCCI’s grading system.

It is one of the most famous professional sports in India and BCCI so far announced four grading systems for Men’s cricketers and three grading systems for women’s cricketers. Top Players in Indian cricket team men's include :

  • Virat Kohli(VK)
  • Rohit Sharma(RS)
  • Jasprit Bumrah(JB)

BCCI’s Grading system for Men’s: BCCI grading system is based on the salary which is further divided into grades as A+,A,B,C with different salary brackets for the cricket players.
